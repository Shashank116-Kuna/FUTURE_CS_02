
# 📘 Phishing Detection & Awareness Report

---

## 1. Introduction

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or financial details.

This report analyzes phishing emails and provides awareness guidelines to prevent such attacks.

---

## 2. Sample Email

### Subject:
⚠️ Urgent: Your Account Will Be Locked

### Email Body:

Dear User,

We noticed suspicious activity on your account.  
To avoid account suspension, please verify your details immediately.

👉 http://secure-account-verify[.]com

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in permanent account lock.

Regards,  
Security Team

---

## 3. Phishing Indicators

| Indicator | Explanation |
|----------|------------|
| Generic Greeting | Uses "Dear User" instead of real name |
| Urgency | Creates panic using account suspension warning |
| Suspicious Link | Fake domain not related to real service |
| No HTTPS | Unsafe link |
| Fake Sender | No verified email address |
| Weak Signature | No company details |

---

## 4. Link Analysis

- URL: http://secure-account-verify[.]com  
- Type: Suspicious / Malicious  
- Risk: Credential Theft  

---

## 5. Risk Classification

🔴 **PHISHING (HIGH RISK)**

---

## 6. How the Attack Works

1. Attacker sends fake email
2. User clicks malicious link
3. Fake website opens
4. User enters login credentials
5. Attacker steals information

---

## 7. Prevention Guidelines

### ✅ Do’s
- Check sender email carefully
- Hover over links before clicking
- Use official websites directly
- Report suspicious emails to IT team

### ❌ Don’ts
- Do not click unknown links
- Do not share passwords or OTPs
- Do not trust urgent threats blindly

---

## 8. Employee Awareness Tips

- Always verify unexpected emails
- Be cautious of urgent messages
- When in doubt, do not click

---

## 9. Conclusion

Phishing attacks rely on human mistakes rather than technical vulnerabilities. Awareness and careful behavior are the best defense.
