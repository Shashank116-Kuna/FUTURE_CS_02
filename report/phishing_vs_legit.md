# Phishing vs Legitimate Email

| Feature | Phishing Email | Legit Email |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| Greeting | Generic | Personalized |
| Link | Suspicious | Official domain |
| Tone | Urgent / Threatening | Informational |
| Sender | Fake | Verified |
| Grammar | Often poor | Professional |

---

## Key Tip:
Always verify before clicking any link.
